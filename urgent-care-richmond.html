Urgent Care in Richmond, TX

Richmond Medical Clinic's urgent care service gives Richmond, TX patients something rare — the speed and accessibility of a walk-in clinic combined with the clinical depth of an established family medicine practice, including on-site diagnostics and physicians who may already know the patient's medical history.

Richmond Medical Clinic — Prompt Walk-In Urgent Care in Richmond, TX

Health problems don't schedule themselves — and Richmond Medical Clinic's Urgent Care in Richmond, TX is designed around that reality. Whether it's a child's sudden ear infection, an adult's sprained wrist, or a respiratory illness that appeared overnight, the clinic's physicians evaluate and treat non-emergency conditions promptly, thoroughly, and at a fraction of the cost of a hospital ER visit.

The difference at Richmond Medical Clinic is knowledge. For established patients, the urgent care physician already has access to their medical history, current prescriptions, known allergies, and chronic conditions — context that leads to more informed clinical decisions, safer prescribing, and a level of personalized care that no generic walk-in clinic can offer.

⚠️ Urgent care vs. emergency room: Richmond Medical Clinic's urgent care is appropriate for non-life-threatening conditions. Patients experiencing chest pain, difficulty breathing, stroke symptoms, severe or uncontrolled bleeding, loss of consciousness, or other emergencies should call 911 or go immediately to the nearest hospital emergency room. For all other sudden medical needs, Richmond Medical Clinic's urgent care team is ready to help.

Conditions Treated at Richmond Medical Clinic's Urgent Care

Respiratory Infections

Colds, coughs, bronchitis, sinus infections, and community-acquired pneumonia represent some of the most common reasons patients seek urgent care at Richmond Medical Clinic. The clinic's physicians evaluate symptoms thoroughly, order appropriate diagnostics using the on-site laboratory, and prescribe effective treatment to resolve respiratory illness as quickly as possible.

Fever & Influenza

High fever, body aches, fatigue, and influenza-like illness require prompt evaluation — particularly in younger children, elderly patients, and individuals with underlying health conditions. Richmond Medical Clinic offers rapid influenza testing and can prescribe antiviral medications when clinically appropriate, reducing both the severity and duration of illness.

Ear, Nose & Throat Conditions

Ear infections, strep throat, tonsillitis, and acute sinusitis are extremely prevalent among both children and adults. Richmond Medical Clinic performs rapid strep testing and otoscopic examinations in-office, delivering quick and accurate diagnoses and prescribing appropriate antibiotic or supportive treatment during the same visit.

Minor Injuries & Wounds

Sprains, strains, minor fractures, lacerations, cuts, and bruising from everyday accidents are evaluated and managed efficiently at Richmond Medical Clinic. The clinic provides wound care and closure, splinting, and referral coordination for imaging when necessary — handling the majority of minor injuries without the need for an emergency room visit.

Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs)

Urinary tract infections cause significant discomfort and can escalate quickly without timely treatment. Richmond Medical Clinic's on-site urinalysis produces rapid results, allowing physicians to diagnose and prescribe treatment for UTIs during the same visit — providing fast relief for patients throughout Richmond, TX.

Skin Conditions, Rashes & Allergic Reactions

Allergic reactions, hives, rashes, insect bites, minor burns, and skin infections can cause considerable discomfort and occasionally signal more serious underlying conditions. Richmond Medical Clinic's physicians conduct careful skin evaluations to ensure accurate diagnosis and deliver effective, targeted treatment.

Gastrointestinal Illness

Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, and acute abdominal discomfort that warrant prompt evaluation are addressed at Richmond Medical Clinic's urgent care. The clinic's team identifies the underlying cause and provides appropriate treatment and supportive care, including rehydration guidance and medication when indicated.

Eye Irritation & Conjunctivitis

Pink eye, eye irritation, and minor eye injuries are evaluated and treated at Richmond Medical Clinic. The clinic's physicians determine whether viral, bacterial, or allergic causes are responsible and prescribe the appropriate treatment course for fast resolution.

Headaches & Migraines

Severe, persistent, or unusual headaches deserve proper clinical evaluation. Richmond Medical Clinic's physicians conduct thorough assessments to identify potential causes and provide effective management for migraine and tension headache presentations among Richmond, TX patients.
