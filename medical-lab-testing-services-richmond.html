Lab Tests Available at Richmond Medical Clinic

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

The complete blood count measures red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets to help diagnose anemia, infections, immune disorders, and blood conditions. Richmond Medical Clinic routinely orders this fundamental test during annual physicals and illness evaluations to establish a clear picture of each patient's blood health.

Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP)

The comprehensive metabolic panel evaluates kidney and liver function, blood glucose levels, electrolytes, and protein levels. Richmond Medical Clinic uses this panel extensively for monitoring patients with diabetes, hypertension, kidney disease, and liver conditions, as well as during routine preventive screenings.

Lipid Panel & Cholesterol Testing

Richmond Medical Clinic's lipid panel measures total cholesterol, LDL, HDL, and triglyceride levels to assess each patient's cardiovascular risk profile. High cholesterol is a leading risk factor for heart disease and stroke — making regular cholesterol screening a core component of the clinic's preventive care program for Richmond, TX patients.

Blood Glucose & HbA1c Testing

Diabetes affects millions of Americans, and early detection is critical to effective management. Richmond Medical Clinic offers both point-of-care blood glucose testing and HbA1c panels — the latter reflecting average blood sugar levels over the preceding two to three months — for comprehensive diabetes diagnosis and ongoing monitoring.

Thyroid Function Tests (TSH, Free T3, Free T4)

Thyroid disorders are among the most common endocrine conditions seen at Richmond Medical Clinic, affecting patients of all ages and genders. The clinic's thyroid panel evaluates TSH, Free T3, and Free T4 levels to diagnose and monitor hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism — conditions that profoundly affect energy, metabolism, mood, and overall health.

Urinalysis

Richmond Medical Clinic's in-office urinalysis provides rapid, non-invasive assessment of urine to detect urinary tract infections, kidney disease, diabetes markers, and other conditions. Results are typically available during the patient's visit, enabling same-appointment diagnosis and treatment.

Rapid Strep & Influenza Testing

For patients presenting with sore throat or flu-like symptoms, Richmond Medical Clinic offers rapid diagnostic testing for streptococcal infection and influenza. Results are available within minutes, allowing the clinic's physicians to prescribe targeted treatment immediately — particularly important for reducing illness duration and preventing complications in vulnerable patients.

Pregnancy Testing

Confidential and accurate pregnancy testing is available to patients at Richmond Medical Clinic, with supportive counseling and clear guidance on next steps provided by the clinic's compassionate care team regardless of results.

STI & Infectious Disease Screening

Richmond Medical Clinic provides discreet, confidential screening for common sexually transmitted infections and other infectious diseases in a respectful, non-judgmental environment. Early detection is essential for effective treatment and preventing further transmission within the community.
