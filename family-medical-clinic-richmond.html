💡 The American Academy of Family Physicians (AAFP) defines family medicine as the medical specialty that provides continuing, comprehensive healthcare for individuals and families — integrating biological, clinical, and behavioral sciences across all ages, genders, and disease types.

Family Medicine Services at Richmond Medical Clinic

Annual Physical Exams & Preventive Care

Richmond Medical Clinic places strong emphasis on prevention as the most effective form of healthcare. Annual physical exams at the clinic include comprehensive health assessments, blood pressure screening, cholesterol evaluation, diabetes screening, age-appropriate cancer screenings, and personalized health counseling. Staying current with preventive visits is the most reliable way to detect health issues early and maintain long-term wellbeing.

Chronic Disease Management

Managing a chronic condition requires consistent, knowledgeable care from a physician who understands the patient's full medical history. Richmond Medical Clinic's family medicine team provides dedicated management for a wide range of chronic conditions, including:

  • Type 2 Diabetes and pre-diabetes monitoring and management
  • Hypertension (high blood pressure) treatment and ongoing monitoring
  • High cholesterol and cardiovascular risk reduction programs
  • Asthma and COPD management and medication review
  • Thyroid disorders including hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism
  • Arthritis and chronic joint pain management
  • Depression, anxiety, and mental health care coordination

Acute Illness Treatment

When patients are feeling unwell, Richmond Medical Clinic offers prompt same-day appointments for acute illness care. The clinic's physicians treat colds, flu, sinus infections, urinary tract infections, skin conditions, and a wide range of sudden health concerns — working efficiently to restore patients' health as quickly as possible.

Women's Health Services

Richmond Medical Clinic provides comprehensive women's health services as part of its family medicine program, including annual wellness exams, Pap smears, breast health evaluations, contraceptive counseling, and menopause management. The clinic takes a whole-person approach to women's health at every stage of life.

Men's Health Services

Richmond Medical Clinic creates a comfortable, judgment-free environment where men can address health concerns proactively. The clinic's men's health services include prostate health screenings, cardiovascular risk assessment, testosterone evaluation, and lifestyle counseling specifically tailored to men's health needs.

Senior & Geriatric Care

Older adult patients at Richmond Medical Clinic receive specialized attention to maintain their health, independence, and quality of life. The clinic's physicians offer comprehensive geriatric assessments, medication management, fall prevention counseling, and coordination of complex care needs for senior patients throughout Richmond, TX.

Pre-Employment & Sports Physicals

Richmond Medical Clinic offers efficient and thorough physical examinations for employment requirements, school enrollment, and sports participation clearance — providing the documentation patients need without unnecessary delays.
