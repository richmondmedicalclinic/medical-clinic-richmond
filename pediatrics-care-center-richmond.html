Pediatric Services Offered at Richmond Medical Clinic

Well-Child Visits & Preventive Care

Routine well-child visits form the backbone of Richmond Medical Clinic's pediatric care program. During these visits, the clinic's physicians perform thorough physical examinations, track growth and developmental milestones, administer vaccinations, and address any parental concerns. These preventive checkups are critical for identifying potential health issues early and ensuring every child in Richmond, TX receives the healthy start they deserve.

Childhood Vaccinations & Immunizations

Richmond Medical Clinic follows the CDC-recommended childhood immunization schedule, protecting young patients from serious, preventable diseases. The clinic's pediatric team takes care to make each vaccination visit as comfortable and reassuring as possible, and remains available to address any questions parents may have about their child's immunization timeline.

Sick Child Visits

When illness strikes, prompt medical attention is essential. Richmond Medical Clinic offers same-day sick visits for a wide range of common childhood conditions, including ear infections, strep throat, respiratory illness, fever, stomach bugs, and skin rashes. The clinic works to see sick children as quickly as possible so that appropriate treatment can begin without delay.

Developmental Screenings

Richmond Medical Clinic's pediatric team conducts comprehensive developmental screenings at each well-child visit to monitor cognitive, behavioral, social, and physical development. Early identification of any developmental concerns allows for timely intervention, which is strongly associated with better long-term outcomes for children in Richmond, TX and surrounding communities.

Adolescent & Teen Health

Teenagers present unique healthcare needs that require a thoughtful, individualized approach. Richmond Medical Clinic provides confidential, respectful care for adolescent patients — including sports physicals, mental health screenings, nutritional guidance, and age-appropriate health counseling — in an environment where teens feel comfortable expressing their concerns.

Newborn Care

The clinic welcomes new families with thorough newborn examinations, feeding guidance, jaundice monitoring, and support for the many questions that accompany life with a new baby. Richmond Medical Clinic is proud to be the healthcare home that guides Richmond, TX families from the very beginning.
